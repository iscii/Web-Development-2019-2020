Welcome to Haunted House!!


Goal: Escape the haunted house!
There are some dangers in this house that might kill you! Try avoid such dangers, and keep safe, and try get out of the creepy house!

There are some hidden hint through out your adventure. They will help you, but you don't necessarily have to find them.

How to navigate: Below the story paragraph, there is several buttons that you can click to either do an action or move on to other room.

These are some of the navigational button:
Click this to go down one floor.
Click this to go up one floor.
Click this to turn left.
Click this to turn right.
This will lead you to the Main Hall. (The first room that you see when you start playing)

Some of these buttons might not show in all of the rooms depending where you are. Also, other buttons other than these ones might also show up!

Other than these navigational button, there are action buttons. These ones might be or might not be on your screen depending on the story. I don't want to tell you what they are because it can be a spoiler!!

Last tip: Think carefully before doing an actions or moving on to other rooms.
