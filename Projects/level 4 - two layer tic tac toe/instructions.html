Welcome to 2-Layer Tic Tac Toe

By Issac Zheng

Instructions
        To begin, choose which "character" to play as - 'x' or 'o'. 'x' always goes first.
        Beat your opponent by claiming three grids in a row, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
        Clicking the Reset button will reset the game and prompt you to choose your character.
Rules
        2 Layers:
                - Players may claim an already occupied grid if it is not locked
                - Players may lock a grid by claiming one that is already occupied by them
        Miscellaneous:
                - X always goes first
                - Wins account for both locked and unlocked grids
                - Players may not claim a grid claimed by the opponent on the previous turn
